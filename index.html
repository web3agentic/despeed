What is DeSpeed?

DeSpeed is the world’s first decentralized network for internet speed verification, powered by real-time blockchain validation. By connecting through a simple protocol, your device measures its internet speed, records its proof on the blockchain, and contributes to a global map of concurrent internet speeds.

This decentralized approach ensures a trustless environment by employing proof-of-speed-based results validation and rewarding genuine users.DeSpeed turns the Internet into a space of shared benefits, where verified ISPs can reward users for providing real-time speed data they need to enhance their service, win-win for everyone!
